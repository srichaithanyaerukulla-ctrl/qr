What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a 2D barcode that stores information like URLs, text, or contact details. Any smartphone camera can scan it instantly — no app needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the QR codes free to use commercially?
Yes. All QR codes generated on QRCraft are completely free — personal or commercial use, no restrictions.
Do QR codes expire?
QR codes generated here are static — they never expire. The code will always point to the exact content you encoded.
What error correction level should I use?
Medium (M) is recommended for most uses. Use High (H) if the QR code will be printed and may get damaged, like on packaging or outdoor signage.
Is my data stored anywhere?
No. QRCraft runs 100% in your browser. Your content is never sent to any server or stored anywhere.
PNG vs SVG — which should I download?
PNG is great for digital use and standard printing. SVG is a vector format — ideal if you need to scale up for large print without losing quality.
